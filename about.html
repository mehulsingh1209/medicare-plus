Our Story

Founded in 2010, MediCare Plus began with a simple mission: to provide quality healthcare products and exceptional service to the people of Birgunj. What started as a small pharmacy has grown into one of the most trusted healthcare providers in the region.

Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, adoption of modern practices, and an unwavering commitment to customer well-being. Today, we serve thousands of customers, providing them with genuine medicines, expert consultation, and reliable healthcare solutions.
